About The Course

Course Description: This course provides an extensive introduction to Python programming, data science, and machine learning, designed to equip participants with the skills necessary to excel in the field. With a total of 140 practical exercises, students will engage in hands-on applications to solidify their understanding of key concepts.
As data science is increasingly recognized as one of the most essential professions in the 21st century, this training covers a broad range of competencies required to become a proficient data scientist. Under the guidance of industry professionals, students will explore real-world applications that enhance their learning experience and facilitate their progression from beginner to advanced levels.
The curriculum focuses on developing a strong foundation in Python programming, emphasizing both the theoretical underpinnings and practical applications. Participants will engage with a variety of topics, including:

-Python Programming
-Data Literacy
-Data Manipulation
-Exploratory Data Analysis
-Data Visualization
-Statistics for Data Science
-Data Preprocessing
-Machine Learning
-Big Data Analytics
-Natural Language Processing and Text Mining
-Twitter Analytics
-Databases and SQL (SQLite)
-Data Science Project Management
